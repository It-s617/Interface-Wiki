Piazza D'Italia
Name: Piazza D'Italia
Designed By: Charles Moore
Year Opened: 1978
Location: New Orleans
Description

Piazza D'Italia is a public area in New Orleans design in recognition of the Italian community’s contribution to the city and has become an iconic part of the city since it was opened to the public.

Analysis of Uses in The Show

Episode 23 The Unconscious Conscious

There is likely no particular meaning to this appearing in the show, beyond the fact this is a very iconic and unique looking plaza.

Both this piazza located in New Orleans and New York are shown in this episode which proves that The Interfaces is not 1 to 1 with the real world and you can likely travel as you wish.
