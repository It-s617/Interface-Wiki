Description of The Interface

The Interface refers to a large amount of Cerebral Electricity under the control of Greetings Robotics. Greetings robotics uses this to create a digital afterlife for people to live in a simulation of the real world presumably before 1943 and The Philadelphia Experiment.




Analysis

What’s It Like in The Interface

The interface seems similar to the real world but more in the way a video game is like the real world rather than a direct simulation.

During episode 23 we see several shots of the landscape and New York these all seem like real places but with less detail, this excluding Henryk’s home which is extremely detailed. To me this makes it seam like the interfaces will eventually be a perfect simulation but currently lacks the resources to perfectly recreate the world.


How Do You Enter the Interface

We see two characters enter the interfaces on permanently the over temporarily I will explain both bellow.

We see in Episode 22 Henryk is taken into The Interface by Cerebral Electricity hands which causes his eyes to glaze over with Cerebral Electricity before he collapses.

In the beginning of Episode 24 we see Mr Greetings execute himself with a mask attached to his faces to capture his cerebral electricity. This show us there is a direct way to be uploaded to The Interfaces, but they may be able to harvest the Cerebral Electricity that is lose in the world and reassemble it into a person.


Where And What Exactly Is the Interface

The Interface is not implied to be a centralised system be an aggregate of a verity of different cerebral electricity container’s being used by greetings robotics. Some of the systems that are likely directly connected to the Interface are KAMI, The Mechanical Saccorhytus and the lighthouse.

The Lighthouse is seen to directly connect to the Interfaces via the cerebral electricity column at the centre of The Lighthouse as Henryk enters the interfaces via the hands that come out from it.

KAMI is implied to be able to directly absorb cerebral electricity monsters though she is never shown to do this on camera.

The Mechanical Saccorhytus has been shown taking cerebral electricity form KAMI and haws also bean shown taking Mr Greetings cerebral electricity via a port.
