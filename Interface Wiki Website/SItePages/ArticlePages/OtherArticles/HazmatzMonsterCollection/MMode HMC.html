How Is The Ghost In The Hazmatz Collection

During the advert we see a shot of a cute cartoon ghost before a it briefly changes to show The Ghost also known as Henryk’s Granddaughter. This is odd as at this point The Ghost should have been uploaded to the interfaces for KAMI to use, I believe that this reviles some information on how greetings robotics operates that I will explain bellow.

The Ghost’s appearances in the advert makes the most seems if The Hazmatz Collection are working with Greetings Robotics, we know that greetings robotics seeks to capture and repurposes all cerebral electrify monsters and its possible that The Hazmatz collection sensitives to sell weakened and largely safe copies of cerebral electricity monsters for profit.

We know that Greetings Robotics is capable of making copies of cerebral electricity monsters and is capable of modifying them as this is reputedly shown in the series.

This would imply that Greetings Robotics has captured multiple Cerebral Electricity Monsters of screen, given that we see nothing else’s of these monsters it’s possible that they are to weak to be useful to KAMI which is how we usually see the monsters captured by Greetings Robotics.

A reason The Ghost may have been used despite being quite powerful is that she is shown to be very good with children as she has a granddaughter, she was raising prior to being taken by Greetings Robotics and she like also raised a child, but no information is given on this.
