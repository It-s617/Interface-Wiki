The Statue of Liberty

This statue depicts a lady dressed in robes holding a torch. This statue was originally a copper colour but has oxidised to blue. This statue was meant to represent freedom and liberty as well as the close relationship between the French and America.

Dreamland

Dreamland is also technically located in New York on a small island called Cony Island, it was bult in 1904 and was in operation until 1911 when it burned down.

The only noticeable differences between the real worlds Dreamland and Interface Dreamland is the addition of The foolish house where Mischief was known to work.

The Lighthouse

The lighthouse is an extremally important location within interfaces which is seemingly where greetings robotics was storing cerebral electricity. This was the location that Henryk and Mischief have their confrontation with greetings robotics in the finale episode.

The location of The Lighthouse is likely somewhere along the Saint Lawrence River as it is the closes places to the east of Montreal where you cannot usually see the over side of the river as shown in episode 22 and episode 24.

I believe that the most likely location for The Lighthouse is somewhere in the middle of the blue line shown on the map below as in episode 13 we see Henryk driving by several rivers which must be near his home as he is capable of cycling to his home from there. We also see in the background of this shot a picture of a city likely Quebec City which would match my theory that the lighthouse is in the rough middle of this line.

Additional it is technically possible that the Lighthouse is located somewhere alongside the New Brunswick cost but I believe this could have been harder to travel to from Montral as they likely would have had to travel through Maine which is across the American border, additionally this doesn’t match the rivers or city seen in episode 13.
