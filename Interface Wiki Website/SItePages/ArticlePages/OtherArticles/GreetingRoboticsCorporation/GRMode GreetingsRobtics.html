Below is an explanation for how the timeline works.


All events will be in chronological order if possible.


If multiple events happen in the same year that will be numbered in the order they occur, explanations below for clarity.

Events that happen within an episode not a flashback will have the episode number after the year.

if the order is unclear or there are no other events then there will be no number to signify what the order they occurred in.

in cases where only the order of some events are clear, the events that have no clear order will share a number.


The date is as specific as it is written, explanations below for clarity.

If a year is stated with no additional information that is the exact year it happened.

If a year is said to be before a year it is that year or any point before.

If a year is said to be after a year it is that year or any point after.

If a year is said to be around a year it is near that year but may be a few years before or after.

Analysis

What Dose Greetings Robotics Mean by Restoration

The stated goal of the Greetings Robotics Corporation is restoration however what this mean is not really explained in the show. I believe that there are three different gaols enclosed in this, I will elaborate bellow.



Meaning One: remove cerebral electricity from the world.

Mr Greetings clearly wants to just completely remove cerebral electricity from the world but knows that he can’t as this seems to be a fundamental part of the universe. I believe that as he knows this his goal has now changed to trying to completely control all the cerebral electricity in the world.



Meaning Two: Removal Of Cerebral Electricity Monsters from The World

Well Mr Greetings cannot fully remove cerebral electricity from the world, but he likely could succeed in capturing and repurposing all the cerebral electricity monsters in the world, this would help to return the world to a state like it was in 1943.



Meaning Three: Creation Of the Interface

One of other clear gaols of greetings robotics is to build The Interfaces a form of digital afterlife, fuelled by cerebral electricity. This is seemingly intended to be a simulation of the world without cerebral electricity. A world without cerebral electricity would represent the ultimate return to normalcy from Mr Greetings prospective.



Analysis of Mr Greetings

Mr Greetings Represents the Past

I believe that both Henryk and Mr Greetings represent the past, I believe that Mr Greetings is meant to represent the old people in the world that refuse to except that the world has moved on from when they were young and are scared of the new world.

This is pretty clear throughout the series especially in Episode 21 Restoration where Mr Greetings admits that a lot of people born after 1943 don’t think there’s anything wrong with the new world and don’t understand what the past was like.



What Species is Mr Greetings

Mr Greetings appear to be a regular human being but we see in episode 21 restoration that during the Philadelphia Experiment that he was transformed into pure cerebral electricity and transformed back into his original form. This would seemingly imply that he is no longer purely human but it is not completely clear.



Analysis of KAMI

KAMI Represents The Loss Of Individuality To Society

Individuality is something that seems particularly important to the series, Mischief is arguably meant to represent individualisation, noticeably in episode 22 quoting The Red Book which is all about individuality.

KAMI how is directly antithetical to Mischief seems to be some form of robot that is powered by the collected Cerebral Electricity of the dead. I think this may be meant to represent the loss of individuality that society often tries to enforce, practically by people who are like Mr Greetings and are obsessed with an idea of how the world is meant to be.



Analysis of The Mechanical Saccorhytus

Why Have KAMI When The Mechanical Saccorhytus Is So Much Stronger

During her fight with The Chef, we see that The Chef was capable of beating KAMI before The Mechanical Saccorhytus using The Ghosts powers appears behind him capturing him. We also see that after KAMI self-destruction The Mechanical Saccorhytus is sent in to capture Mischief, this leads to the question why Greetings Robotics needed KAMI.

I think this is simply because KAMI is easier to control and can be relied upon while The Mechanical Saccorhytus is unreliable. When The Mechanical Saccorhytus is in his baby form he is massively emotionally and seams easily distracted likely meaning that he is not reliable for capturing other Cerebral electricity monsters.
