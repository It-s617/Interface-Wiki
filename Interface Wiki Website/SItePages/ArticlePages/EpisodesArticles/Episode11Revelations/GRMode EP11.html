Artworks

Christina's World

To understand what this painting meanes for the show it is important to understand the origin of the painting. This painting displays the neighbour of the painter Christina crawling through a filed this is because she has a degenerative muscle disease that prevented her from being able to walk. She chose not to uses a wheelchair and preferred to craw everywhere inspiring the painting.

Well at faces value this painting comes off as sad knowing that she chooses not to use a wheelchair recontextualise it to more of a picture of defiance, with Christina choosing to do things the way she prefers instead of how others may expect her to.

In my opinion this painting has two different interpretations by the characters Mischief and Henryk in this episode.

Henryk seams to take the painting at face value as a futile and meaningless struggle this likely being influenced by his depression and memories of his family which this painting seams to remined him off.

Mischief however seemingly understands the original context of the painting that being a woman choosing to live her life her way no matter what offers think. Mischief actually directly compares himself to the Christina when talk about how he can change colours but choses to be this way.

It is worth noting that Mischief is definitely telling the truth about being able to change colours as we see him do this in multiple episodes like in the sun of man where he turns into a sunflower, however this is not common for him.
