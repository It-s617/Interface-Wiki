Description

This episode takes place at a United Nations meeting where KAMI mark one is announced and demonstrated.
This episode introduces the primary antagonists of the series Joseph Greetings and KAMI as well as introducing the Greetings Robotics corporation.

Summary

Opening

This episode opens with a shoot of the Greetings Robotics blimp called The Mechanical Saccorhytus before panning down to the United Nations Headquarters, during this scene we hear Mr Greeting monologing about the Cerebral Electricity in a clearly negative manner.

Mr Greetings Monologue

Mr Greetings talks about how since 1943 Cerebral Electricity has cased ghost stories and myths to become more than just superstition but a horrifying reality, during this segment we see Mr Greetings and the crowed at the UN headquarters.

Mr Greetings then discuses Cerebral Electricity how it was not a foreign substance but something that had been revealed to humanity as a result of The Philadelphia Experiment, during this segment we see Mr Greetings talking before we see a vat of cerebral electricity and some scientists experimenting on KAMI.

Mr Greeting then discusses the various scientists, engineers, nations, and corporate sponsors that have helped him that help his company protects society form celebre electricity, during this scene we see a mask being lowered onto KAMI.

We see a sign saying that Greetings robotics is working with the United Nations counter-terrorism committee. Finally, Mr Greetings announces that Greetings Robotics has developed an android call KAMI, generation one. KAMI teleports above the stage before floating down to the ground with the crowd applauding.

Ending

There are several angles of KAMI shown before a shot of her hand forming a fist is shown and the episode ends.

Notes

This episode shows that the united nation counter-terrorism committee is working in partnership with Greetings robotics which would imply that the actions of the Cerebral Electricity Monsters are considered an act of terrorism by most governments. (speculation)

This is the first video where Joseph Greetings, KAMI, and Greetings Robotics.

This video has the clearest shot of the symbol on KAMI’s back which means “god” in Japanese, God in Japanese translates to KAMI.
