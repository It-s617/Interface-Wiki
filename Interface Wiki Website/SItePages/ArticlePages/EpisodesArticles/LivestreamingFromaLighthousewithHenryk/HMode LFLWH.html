Title: Livestreaming From a Lighthouse with Henryk
Released (DMY): 26/1/2021
Video Type: Short, Livestream
Synopsis: Mischief just checkin' in on ya.

Summary

The short opens on a shot of Mischief before he welcomes the live stream and claims that he was everything under control.

Mischief mentions he is hanging out with buddy Henryk before panning over to him. Henryk is seen standing still with cerebral electricity covering his eyes, Mischief claims that Henryk is tripping balls and has probably taken mushrooms.

Mischief remember that The Mechanical Saccorhytus is floating nearby, before the camera pans over to show him.

Mischief restates that he has everything under control and that he doesn’t need help before closing out the livestream, Mischief is clearly lying about being in control.

Notes

It is not clear if Mischief is meant to be contacting a specific character but its most like he is just livestreaming publicly.

This short contains one of three references to the fact theta Henryk doesn’t eat through all of interface.
