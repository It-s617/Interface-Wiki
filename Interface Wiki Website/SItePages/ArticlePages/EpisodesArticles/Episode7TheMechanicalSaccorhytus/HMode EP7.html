Episode 7 The Mechanical Saccorhytus

Synopsis: Mr. Greetings speaks his thoughts on evolution, and why the universe is the way it is. What is he trying to accomplish? Can you find the hidden messages?

Summary

Opening and Mr Greetings speech

The episode starts on a view of the starts slowly focusing in on earth as Mr Greetings talks about the formation of the starts and earth.

The shot then changes to a beach with a boat on it as Mr Greetings starts to talk about the formation and evolution of life.

We then see pictures of the deep see and he discusses how he believes that atoms over time will try to become more complex, pondering why as the shot refocuses on the stars before The Mechanical Saccorhytus, greetings robotics blimp is seen to float by.

Onboard The Mechanical Saccorhytus

We see Mr greeting looking at a screen that displays info on the blimp and KAMI. KAMI is seen T-posing in a docking area well seemingly connecting to the ship to be “degauss” which usually refers to the removal of water, magnetic, energy or data from a system, in KAMI’s case it is most likely Cerebral Electricity being removed.

KAMI’s mask then begins to disengage and we see some kind of port attach to wear it usually would be before a large amount of Cerebral Electricity is seen to go through the port and connect to the internal structure of The Mechanical Saccorhytus which is shown to have a brain and eyes and even a mouth.

The Ghost’s hand is shown as well as hex code, the hex code translating too ULTIMATE COMPLEXITY IS THE FINAL DESTINATION, INCOMING TRANSMISSION INCOMPLETE, GENE SEQUENCING UNKNOWN NUCLEOTIDES, GR DISCOVERY: COPYING DATA AND ORIENTATION.

The scene then shows more scans of the blips brain before an image of The Ghost is shown being scanned as Mr greeting is watching and also seemingly being scanned.

Ending

We see a lighthouse blinking in the background as a whale briefly appears form the water and the episode ends as a final shot of The Mechanical Saccorhytus is shown.

Hex Code Meaning

ULTIMATE COMPLEXITY IS THE FINAL DESTINATION

This likely correlates to what Mr Greetings is talking about and how the creatures are reaching a stat of ultimate complexity where they cannot get more complex.

INCOMING TRANSMISSION INCOMPLETE

This stats that somehow the data KAMI is sending somehow isn’t complete this isn’t elaborated on and seemingly has no consequences so in my opinion I think this is flavour text.

GENE SEQUENCING UNKNOWN NUCLEOTIDES

This implies that it is breaking down the giant sequence of The Ghost and repurposing them which would implies that all Cerebral Electricity Monster have a biological component and are not purely magic, spiritual, or made of Cerebral Electricity.

GR DISCOVERY: COPYING DATA AND ORIENTATION

This implies that The Ghost is being copied and reorientated to serve Greetings Robotics. I believe that this leads to the destruction of the original Ghost, but this is not made clear in the series.
