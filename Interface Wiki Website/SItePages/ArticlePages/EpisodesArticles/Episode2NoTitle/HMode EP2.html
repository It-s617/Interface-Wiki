This episode is a continuation of episode one which focuses on Mischief and Henryk traveling through the streets of Montreal to the Montreal Museum of Fine Art.

Summary

Opening

This episode starts directly after the first, Mischief transforms into his bus form and directs Henryk to bord him and ensure him that he does not intend to harm him.

Traveling on the Mischief bus

Mischief offers Henryk the opportunity to pick their destination, but Henryk dose not reply so Mischief decides their destination stating, “If you don’t decide, I will… like I’ve done to so many others.”

During this scene he see some scenes of Montreal’s notable locations. we see Turcot Interchange, Montreal’s Olympic stadium, Mount Royal Cross, and the Montreal Museum of Fine Art. noticeable all of these buildings seem infected with Cerebral Electricity.

Ending

Mischief in his bus form arrives at the Montréal Museum of Fine Art and changes into his suit form appearing around Henryk as a pink version of his normal suit. As soon as the transformation ends there is a title cared for the series and the video ends.

Notes

The building in Montreal suggests that this episode takes place sometime in the 2010s around the time of the episode’s releases.
