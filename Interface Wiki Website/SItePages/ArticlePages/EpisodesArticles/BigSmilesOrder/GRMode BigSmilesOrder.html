Big Smile’s Order
Title: Big Smile’s Order
Released (DMY): 22/3/18
Video Type: Short

Summary

Mischief in his car form is seen to pull up to the McDonalds drive through.

Mischief orders Two number 9’s, A number 9 large, A number 6 with extra dip, A number 7, Two number 45’s, one with cheese, and a large soda.

Mischief lets out his oversized tongue with money on it, telling the casher to keep the change.

Notes

This short is a joke based on Mischief occasionally being known as big smile which is the seam name as a popular Grand Theft Auto character.
Big smile has a famous line where he makes an absurdly long fast food order similar to what we see in this video.


This short is implied to take places during the events of the Interface series presumably slightly before the events of Episode 13.


This short contains one of three references to the fact that Henryk doesn’t eat through all of Interface.
