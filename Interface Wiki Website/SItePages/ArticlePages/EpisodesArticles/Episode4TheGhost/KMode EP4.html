Description

This episode focuses on The Ghost and the little girl and introduces a mystery of who these characters are and what The Ghosts relationship with Mischief is.

This episode introduces The Ghost, Henryks great-granddaughter and Henryk’s family.

Summary

Opening

The episode opens with The Ghost and Henryk’s great-granddaughter, who is seen to peacefully sleep well cuddling with The Ghost, who is watching TV which is shown a series of random faces commonly associated with Cerebral Electricity.

The Ghosts Memory

The scene then changes to a memory of the past we see a car filled with members of Henryk’s family including his great-granddaughter being submerged in water, while Mischief and The Ghost watch from the riverbank.

Cerebral Electricity is seen to rise from the river and is consumed by Mischief. The Ghost is then seen to use their hand power to raise Henryk’s great-granddaughter from the wreckage before the memory ends and Henryk’s great-granddaughter is shown to wake up.

KAMI’s Attack

KAMI is seen floating from the Greetings Robotic blimp towards the building that The Ghost and child are in. the child notices KAMI floating towards the building and runs to The Window looking scared. KAMI then uses her powers to remove the walls of the building before somehow absorbing The Ghost

Ending

As The Ghost is being absorbed the child tries to hold The Ghost’s hand but fails as she floats away, she then begins to cry as the episode ends.

Artworks

The Creation of Adam

This is meant to be a Reference to The Creation of Adam, however there is little correlation in meaning or even apparitions other than the focuses on hands. Unfortunately, I have no interpretation for what this could mean and primarily think this was a simple reference.

Notes

Given that The Ghost does not seem to be causing any harm it is unclear why KAMI would attack her. There are two common theories as to why, Greetings robotics may be targeting all forms of Cerebral Electricity monsters regardless of threat posed or that The Ghost is posing a threat to society in an unseen way.

This episode introduces The Ghost, Henryk’s great-granddaughter, and Henryk’s Family.

This is the first episode of the series to feature no dialogue.
