Description

This episode focuses on KAMI being repaired, as well as the apartment complex damaged in here attack on The Chef.

Episode 18 Rebuilding
Episode Number: 18
Title: Rebuilding
Released (DMY): 1/2/20
Video Type: Interface Part 2
Synopsis: KAMI is not dead but was being rebuilt. It has learned new tricks from those it had taken from.

Summary

Opening

The episode opens on a shot of KAMI’s interfaces will she is being repaired. KAMI is shown to have been updated to mark two this is presumably because she took heavy damage in her fight with The Chef. We see a verity of information about the Cerebral Electricity KAMI apparently has access to “Hand Codename”, “LUNG CODENAME” and “HEART CODENAME”.

We see her get retinal data from “Akkorokamui” aka The Chef and an image of Henryk and Mischief loads leading to them being labelled KAMI’s new target.

Rebuilding The Flats

A city street is shown with Cerebral Electricity filling the sky, we then see the flats where The Ghost was staying as a ton of her hands appear with Henryk’s great-granddaughter watching.

The hands use Cerebral Electricity panels to cover the building before we see it transformed into a new building much higher quality than it previous was.

Lastly, we see one of the hands give a thumbs up displaying the grating robotics compony moto.

Ending

We see a scene of Henryk’s great-granddaughter walking down the street as the hands are seemingly recalled to the Greeting Robotics’ blimp before we see some of them make a billboard announcing that Mischief and Henryk are wanted individuals.

The scene ends with The Ghost appearing before Henryk’s great-granddaughter before the episode suddenly ending.

Analysis

KAMIs Interface information

Hand likely referring to The Ghost and her information is listed as CODENAME: SHIVA, SOLVED: PROTECTION, TRANSFORMATION, DESTRUCTION.

Shiva is a destruction goddess from Hindu culture click here for the Wikipedia article on her

Lung likely refers to The Wind their information is “GA-OH, SOLVED: TRACE ELEMENTS ONLY, STATUE: UNKNOWN, this implies she has little access to The Wind as he likely hasn’t been captured yet.

Ga-oh is a wind god from Iroquois mythology click here for the Wikipedia article on him

Lastly there’s Heart which definitely refers to The Chef the information is listed as CODENAME: AKKOROKAMUI, SOLVED: INTUITION AND CHROMOPHORES. Chromophores are molecules that have the ability to absorb and emit light possible allowing for KAMI to transform colours or see in colour.

Akkorokamui is an Octopus-like monster from Ainu Folklore click here for the Wikipedia article on them.

Notes

Henryk’s great-granddaughter does not appear distressed implying that she may already be in contact with Greetings Robotic and The Ghost at this point.
