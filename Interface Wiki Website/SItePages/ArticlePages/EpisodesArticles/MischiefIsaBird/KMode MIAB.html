This short was made to help announces the Mischief bird plush. We see Mischief talk to a dirt dumpster cat and The Hazmatz Spokesperson.

Mischief Is a Bird
Title: Mischief Is a Bird
Released (DMY): 5/1/20
Video Type: Short, Advertisement

Summary

The short opens on a shot of an alley way with a dumpster cat in it before Mischief suddenly pops out and tells that cat that he had caught him in the dumpster.

Mischief suddenly realises the creature is a cat and things he knows something that it may want to see.

The Hazmatz Spokesperson tells Mischief that he has already told him not to bring dumpster animal into the studio and Mischief replies saying that h is the dumpster animal not the cat.

Mischief realises that The Hazmatz Spokesperson is in pain and asks if he is ok, The Hazmatz Spokesperson then claims that he likely has intestinal worms and won’t be able to film the commercial today.

Mischief says that he doubts that The Hazmatz Spokesperson has intestinal worms as he knows a lot about intestinal worms, Mischief then offers to go inside of The Hazmatz Spokesperson to check but is largely ignored by The Hazmatz Spokesperson how hobbles of to go to the hospital.

Mischief starts talking to the cat again and claims that The Hazmatz Spokesperson hasn’t been the same since he had his leg surgically moved to the side of his head.

Mischief moves on and directed the cat to look inside a garage door.

The promo for the Mischief bird push is played.
