This episode is about Henryk and Mischief taking a ride down a country road and discuss their desires.

Episode 13 The Right Direction
Episode Number: 13
Title: The Right Direction
Released (DMY): 18/5/19
Video Type: Interface Part 2
Synopsis: Henryk and Mischief head east in search of Henryk's lost granddaughter.

Summary

Opening

The episode opens on a shot of a country road with a city in the far distance presumable Montreal or Québec City, the scene changes to focuses on a picture of an owl followed by a shot of the same owl flying over a river.

The scene then changes to show the owl outside the widow of Mischief in his car form with Henryk sitting on the passenger seat smoking a cigarette. The episode cuts to as wide shot of a forest with a road in it with the duo are driving on before a scene of an owl sitting in a tree as the duo drive off.

Convection in the car

We see a close up of Mischief in his car form before he transforms his faces to be on the gear shifting stick inside the car so he can talk to Henryk. Mischief asks Henryk about how meany cigarettes he has in his coat claiming that they spontaneously appear.
Mischief then warns Henryk that he does not have any ash trays and does not believe that Henryk will litter, claiming that he has gotten into a conundrum.

The conversation takes a more serious turn as Mischief says he is assuming that they are still on the right direction as Henryk hasn’t said anything, unsurprisingly getting no reply.

Mischief than changes subject to talk about how it was suspicious for him to have known the secret of Henryk’s great-granddaughter surviving all while trying to figure out what Henryk cared about.

Mischief claims to be running low on gas before suddenly changing into a unicycle though still moving at the speed of the car, after a few seconds the pair crash after running over a twig well Henryk dose appear scared, he perfectly lands without injury.

Ending

Mischief apologies to Henryk, claiming that he cannot always help himself. Mischief says that that’s something they may be able to help each other with before changing into his bicycle form, ending the episode.

Notes

This episode confirms that The Lighthouse and Henryk’s home is east of Montreal.

This episode gives to large hints too how Henryk’s incorrigibility may work. The first is that Henryk’s cigarettes are mentioned to appear spontaneously which implies that even his cloths and positions on his person will stay the same. The second is that when he falls form Mischief in his unicycle form, Henryk lands perfectly and is completely unharmed if slightly dazed. This encourages the idea that Henryk is more than immortal and actual entirely incorrigible.
