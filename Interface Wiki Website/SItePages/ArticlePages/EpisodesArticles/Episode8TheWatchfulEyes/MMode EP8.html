Episode 8 The Watchful Eyes
Episode Number: 08
Title: The Watchful Eyes
Released (DMY): 3/8/18
Video Type: Interface Part 1
Synopsis: Mischief comes to visit Henryk in the hospital, and he notices some strange things in the medical records.

Summary

Opening

This episode opens on a shot of a city, Montreal. A pink version of the Goddess of Liberty statue is shown with a Cerebral Electricity halo as well as the Montreal Olympic Stadium which is zoomed out of until we see it through The Window of Henryk’s hospital window.

Television Adverts

In Henryk’s hospital room we see a tv playing an advert from Greetings Robotics. the advert shows a series of random faces before displaying a message saying SUCERE beneath the watchful eyes, followed up by the grating robotics logo and their tagline “Keeping Communities Safe.”

A second advert is shown for Hazmatz Monster Collection a business that seems to sell Cerebral Electricity Monster to children. Several monsters are shown most noticeable The Ghost is shown which may imply that this company works with Greetings Robotics and that may imply the monsters sold are entirely tame and safe despite appearances, though there is a warning that they may be dangerous this could just be for marketing.

Mischief Is a Bird

Mischief suddenly appears in his bird form saying tweet tweet and explains to Henryk that he is attempting to be a bird but is not good at it yet.

Mischief then decides to check out Henryk’s medical record discovering that his name is Henryk Niebieski and that he was born in 1910 the medical records state that Henryk fainted and was lightheaded as well as questing his date of birth claiming he may have stolen this identity, though this is meant to be clearly false as we know Henryk just doesn’t age.

Mischief is surprised by Henryk’s age and claims that he is hiding something before making a joke about how smooth his skin is for his age transforming the space around him into a health magazine. Mischief then warns Henryk that if he stays here any longer that he will likely become a medical experiment and turns into a parachute so Henryk can escape out The Window.

Ending
