The Condemnation
Title: The Condemnation
Released (DMY): 28/3/24
Video Type: Short

Summary

The short opens on a wide shot of a farm in the rain, noticeable the skies are grey.

We hear a voice that sounds like Mischief say, “This is the condemnation,”

We see an interior shot of a body crawling across the floor. Through The Windows we can see the sky is red and it is raining.

Mischief continues “that light is come into this world,”

We see a shot of a hand getting dragged across the wall.

Mischief concludes “and men loved darkness rather than light, because there deeds were evil.”

We see a man in an Oni mask, lift up his mask reviling pure cerebral electricity.

The video suddenly ends.

Analysis

Note: Given that there’s less than one minute of screen time for The Oni there’s a very good chances any analysis is going to be wrong.



Two Storms Theory

In the opening shot we see a wide view of the farm and can clearly see that the sky is grey which would usually imply that the scene is taking places at some point before 1943 when the Philadelphia experiment acquired.

In all the rest of the scenes however it is clear that the sky is a dark red which seams normal for the post 1943 world.

This makes it seem to me that this video may show two different storms happening at the same places but at different times. This would explain why the farm looks well maintained in the opening shot but is clearly dilapidated in the other scenes.

It is not clear why this would be important to show the farm at two different points but given that this video seems to be an extract of a large screen, this likely just means that we haven’t seen why it is important yet.



Who is The Oni

The Oni appears to have a connection to KAMI, I believe that The Oni is meant to be a transformed version of KAMI after her self-destruction ion episode 24 The abyss.

The Oni is an incredibly unique creature even within the interface’s universe. As form what we can tell The Oni is the result of KAMI transforming into a Cerebral Electricity Monster, KAMI being a robot containing a large amount of cerebral electricity presumably from humans.

Given that cerebral electricity monsters seem to have forms heavily based on their memory’s and perspectives on the world this would likely lead to The Oni being incurably unique as they are a composite of many people cerebral electricity that was being contained by KAMI.
