Hex Code Meaning

This hex code appears shortly before the KAMI fight.

KAMI CAPTURE.CONFIG

This means that KAMI is loading a program for catching Cerebral Electricity Monsters.

CE DETECTED

This means that Cerebral Electricity has been detected.

WARNING: CE CORRUPTION DETECTED

This means that the Cerebral Electricity has been corrupted the meaning of this is not explained but likely just means it is being controlled by a Cerebral Electricity Monster.

WARNING: KAMI SYSTEMS COMPROMISED

This could mean that KAMI is suffering from a glitch due to an attack or the presences of The Chef.

KAMI.PT.783921706013 STATUS: SEARCHING AREA

This likely means that KAMI is searching the area for The Chef.

WARNING: TARGET HOSTILE

This confirms that they know The Chef will not come peacefully.

INITIATING GHOST.PROTECTOR

This shows that KAMI can summon The Ghost to protect herself.
