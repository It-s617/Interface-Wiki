Description

This episode shows Henryk and Mischief on their ways to the light house in search of Henryk’s granddaughter, as Mischief alludes to the secrets of his past.

Episode 19 Entanglement
Episode Number: 19
Title: Entanglement
Released (DMY): 12/3/20
Video Type: Interface Part 2
Synopsis: Mischief and Henryk approach the lighthouse in search for clues on the whereabouts of Henryk's great granddaughter. Mischief may not be telling the whole story about his past and what he witnessed, but he knows Henryk will discover the answers on his own and bestows him the freedom of autonomy.

Summary

Opening

The episode opens on Henryk playing the harmonica on a couch in his home, before Mischief in his fat frog form is seen hopping towered him. The scene changes back to Henryk, and we see he has laid down to sleep with Mischief transforming into a blanket for him to use.

Henryk and Mischief conversation by the USS impermanence

Mischief is seen traveling as giant ball of string calling this form of movement quantum entanglement and claiming that if observed the form will collapse. Henryk proceeds to look at Mischief causing the form to collapse.

Mischief begins to ask Henryk what they are looking for in the lighthouse as Henryk’s great-granddaughter is obviously not still waiting there after so meany years. Mischief asks Henryk if he thinks she would recognise Henryk and if Henryk would recognise her.

Mischief asks Henryk how even when the facade of flesh and memory changes he remains so capable, likely referring to Henryk’s ability to recognise his granddaughter even as she grows. Mischief then says that if Henryk changes like he had Mischief s recognise himself.

Mischief then stats that even across the world Henryk would immediately know something is wrong, showing a scene of Henryk’s great-granddaughter riding in The Ghost’s hand.

Mischief warns Henryk that if he knows everything, he did then Henryk would collapse, adding that he will learn this on his own. A scene is shown The Ghost talking to Henryk’s granddaughter with Henryk’s great-granddaughter in the background, this is followed by a shot of Henryk’s grate-granddaughter faces and then a shot of Henryk’s granddaughter staring at the screen, likely are where Henryk’s great-granddaughter is standing.

Ending

The scene changes to The Lighthouse surrounded by Cerebral Electricity ending the episode.

Notes

This episode provides prof that Henryk may be purely human as Mischief says that Henryk has not changed like him. This is not adequate proof that Henryk is fully human as we know his form is similar to a human regardless.
