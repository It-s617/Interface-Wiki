The Creation of Adam
Title: The Creation of Adam
Made by: Michelangelo
Year Made: 1512
Type: Painting
Description

This painting shows God reaching down to Adam to bestow the gift of knowledge on him.

God is shown in the sky surrounded by angels in a pose which resembles a brain, while Adam is lying on the ground naked.

The figures are shown to be touching their hands together.

Original Meaning of The Work

There has been much discussion on the meaning of this painting and what is presented in it. I will go over what I believe the most relevant meanings are here.

The most common interpretation is this depicts go bestowing man with intelligence, God is depicted in a shape resembling a brain and Adam is depicted as naked and primal with a noticeable vacuous look on his faces.

Another interpretation is that this is meant to depict the creation of Eve, Adam is noticeable shown to have an extra rib commonly referred to as Eve’s rib. Additional God is shown with many figures around him including a woman who is staring at an Adam, who is suspected to be Eve.

Analysis of Uses in The Show

Episode 4 The Ghost.

In this episode after KAMI is seen to destroys the wall of the building, The Ghost is seen floating away as Henryk’s Greet-Granddaughter tries to reach out to her but fails. I believe this scene is meant to be a reference to The Creation of Adam.

The hands depicted in the scene are likely meant to be a reference to the painting however I am not aware of any deeper meaning to the paintings uses here. It is entirely possible that there is a deeper meaning to this I have missed due to the meany interpretations of the painting, but I have not seen any that seem to match its use in the show.
