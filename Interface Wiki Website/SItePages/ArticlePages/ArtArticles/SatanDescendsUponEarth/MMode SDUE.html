Satan Descends Upon Earth
Title: Satan Descends Upon Earth
Made by: Gustave Doré
Year Made: 1866
Type: Painting
Description

The painting focuses on a winged wan falling from the sky.

The man is surrounded by clouds with some stars in the background.

The man has a beam of light illuminating him.

This painting is in black and white.

Original Meaning of The Work

The original painting was meant to depict Satan’s fall from heaven after he was banished by God for being sinful and impure. It may be important to remember that Christians consider the first sin to have been the knowledge of good an evil.

Analysis of Uses in The Show

Episode 21 Restoration

I believe that this painting was meant to depict that Mischief has gain a higher level of understanding of the universe and lost his “purity”.

This would correlate to what we see in “bear witness” as the egg is often used as a symbol of purity in Christion art and Mischief is seen breaking through the egg which could imply that his transformation made him impure in some ways.
