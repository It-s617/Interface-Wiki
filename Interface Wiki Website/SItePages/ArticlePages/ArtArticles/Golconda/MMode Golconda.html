Description

This painting depicts many men floating in the air, wearing a black suit and bowler hat reminiscent of The Son of Man.

The buildings in the Background are meant to represent the fort of Golconda.

Original Meaning of The Work

Golconda was meant to represent how paintings are false reality of their subjects and can be used in a deceptive manner. in this case it is obviously represented by the verity of floating men but in his other works this appears in many other forms, an example being The Human Condition which shows a painting of a painting in such a perspective that you can barely tell what is what, shown on the right.

Analysis of Uses in The Show

Episode 23 The Unconscious Conscious

It is possible that the false realty of a painting idea is represented in the original painting transfers to the series as an allegory for The Interfaces being a false reality but it is more likely just used as a references to The Son Of Man and because Mr Greetings is talking about the multiverse at the time.
