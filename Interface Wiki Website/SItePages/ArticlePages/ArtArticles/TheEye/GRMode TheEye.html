The Eye
Title: The Eye
Made by: David Altmejd
Year Made: 2011
Type: Statue
Location: Montreal
Description

This statue seemingly represents an angel, but their forms is twisted having a hole in their chest as well as a wrapping around there faces.

Original Meaning of The Work

From what I have read it seems this painting may be externalist but none of the sources I found make it practically clear what the painting represents and how.

I believe that the existential interpretation for this painting may come from the twisted appearance of the angle depicted in the Work, most noticeable having a giant hole in its chest.

Analysis of Uses in The Show

Episode 5 The Son of Man

This is compered to both Mischief and Henryk.

There is no description for the statue given in the description other than that the statue is made of bronze, additionally the statue is not focused on in the episode over than when Mischief is wrapped around it, It is for these reasons I believe that this statue was only meant to be set dressing for this episode.
