Nighthawks
Title: Nighthawks
Made by: Edward Hopper
Year Made: 1942
Type: Painting
Description

The painting depicts a street view of an American dinner.

Four figures are depicted in the painting, a man in a suit and hat, a couple in the background and a chef.

Original Meaning of The Work

This was a painting made in 1942 by Edward Hopper, it was intended to be a portrayal of an American city in 1942 but the writer admitted that they accidentally captured the loneliness of modern cities.

Analysis of Uses in The Show

Episode 24 The Abyss

At first this scene may seem depressing but in ever version there is no actual cause for loneliness the characters are surrounded by people and in the show, we see Henryk is with his daughter.

Compering the dark and cold outside to the light and presumably warm interior of the dinner could imply that Henryk is literally turning his back to the dark parts of his life.
