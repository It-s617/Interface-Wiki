Sunrise By the Ocean
Title: Sunrise by the Ocean
Made by: Vladimir Kush
Year Made: 1996
Type: Painting
Description

This painting depicts a large egg with a crack down the middle.

There is a river in the middle of the paintings.

The sun in the middle of the painting is meant to represent the yoke.

There are several figures depicted in the painting on is seen leaving a boat the rest are seen around scaffolding holding up the egg.

Original Meaning of The Work

This painting was made by Vladimir Kush in 1996 it represents the creation of new life with a verity of different artistic metaphors like the yolk and the sun. This work is similar to Geopoliticus Child Watching the Birth of The New Man by Salvador Dali, Salvador Dali is a common inspiration for Vladimir Kushâ€™s paintings.

Analysis of Uses in The Show

Episode 23 The Unconscious Conscious

In the show the inside of the egg is shown to be a pink blob that then turns into a brain covered in Cerebral Electricity this is likely meant to represent Mischief who was shown as the new man in the painting Geopoliticus Child Watching the Birth of The New Man, in a very similar position.

Essentially, I think this just restates the point of the meaning of Geopoliticus Child Watching the Birth of The New Man in episode 6 that Mischief represents The New Man and progress in the world which Mr Greetings is in stark opposition of.
