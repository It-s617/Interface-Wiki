The Statue of Liberty
Title: The Statue of Liberty
Made by: Frédéric Auguste Bartholdi
Year Opened: 1886
Type: Statue
Location: New York
Description

This statue depicts a lady dressed in robes holding a torch.

This statue was originally a copper colour but has oxidised to blue.

Original Meaning of The Work

This statue was meant to represent freedom and liberty as well as the close relationship between the French and America.

Analysis of Uses in The Show

This is a very famous landmark in New York. I do not believe that there is any meaning to this statue belong that. Noticeable this statue is not featured in the credits as an artwork as it is more known as a landmark than an artwork.

This is not the only statue we see that’s been changed to pink implying that this was a common change Post-The Philadelphia Experiment.
