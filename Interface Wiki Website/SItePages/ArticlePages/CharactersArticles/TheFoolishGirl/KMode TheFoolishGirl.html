Personal Timeline

This segment will be going over all the events of Henryk’s family in order. I will do this as extensively as possible so some speculation will be included but this will be shown as speculation to the best of my ability.

Below is an explanation for how the timeline works.


All events will be in chronological order if possible.


If multiple events happen in the same year that will be numbered in the order they occur, explanations below for clarity.

Events that happen within an episode not a flashback will have the episode number after the year.

if the order is unclear or there are no other events then there will be no number to signify what the order they occurred in.

in cases where only the order of some events are clear, the events that have no clear order will share a number.


The date is as specific as it is written, explanations below for clarity.

If a year is stated with no additional information that is the exact year it happened.

If a year is said to be before a year it is that year or any point before.

If a year is said to be after a year it is that year or any point after.

If a year is said to be around a year it is near that year but may be a few years before or after.



Before 1893 The Foolish Girl is born.

Justification

We know that The Foolish Girl was working for dreamland in 1911 and appeared to be an adult so that means he was likely born at least 18 years before 1911.


Between 1904-1911: The Foolish Girl joined the Foolish house in dreamland.

Justification

We know that The Foolish Girl was working for Dreamland when it burned down meaning he must have been working there before hand, as he is the head on the building it is likely he has there from the beginning, meaning 1904.


1911: Proto-Mischief and The Foolish Girl take a bow on the stage of The Foolish House.

1911: The same day Proto-Mischief retires Dreamland burns down.

1911: The Foolish Girl likely leaves the entertainment industry after Dreamland burns down.

Justification

At one point in Episode 21 Mischief says "The year was 1911. Two comedians take a bow, 54 years too soon." This references the painting the two comedians.

This all implies that Proto-Mischief retired from dreamland and did not leave because of the fire. However, we also see Proto-Mischief watching the fire well dressed in his comedian outfit, implying that he retires the day of the fire.

The Ringmaster appears noticeably upset during the scene he is shown in Episode 21 this may imply that he and Proto-Mischief had been having problems and one of them may have purposely or accidently set fire to dreamland. This is contradicted by reality where the fire was an accident.
