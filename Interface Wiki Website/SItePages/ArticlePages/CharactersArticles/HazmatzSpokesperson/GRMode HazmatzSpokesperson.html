Physical Appearance

The Hazmatz Spokesperson appears to be a man dressed in clown cloths with one leg moved to the sides of his head.


Personality

In adverts his personality comes of as friendly and charming but this may just be as part of the performance.

In the advert "Mischief is a bird" he seems a lot more down and in pain Mischief implies some of this may be due to him having had one of his legs surgically moved to the side of his head, but it could also be because he has intestinal worms.
