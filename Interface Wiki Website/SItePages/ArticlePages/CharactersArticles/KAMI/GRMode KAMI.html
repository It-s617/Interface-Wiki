Description

Physical Appearance

KAMI is a white and black robot with wires coming out the back of her head that resembles dreadlocks, her chest is covered by what appears to be a ribcage with cerebral electricity behind it, at the centre of her ribcage is a small heart. On her back there is a Japanese’s symbol that means God or KAMI in Japanese’s.

KAMI’s mask has to bright red circles on her checks, bright red lips and to holes for her to see through.



Personality

KAMI is a robot that contains cerebral electricity if she has a personality, she doesn’t show it externally, beyond occasionally staring at her mask.

Powers And Abilities

KAMI has the ability to absorb the cerebral electricity of others, this granted her the memories and abilities of anybody she absorbs.

Her original powers where seemingly flying, telekinesis and the ability to destroy and rebuild physical objects. She is also likely capable of anything a regular human can do but we only see her fighting.

KAMI absorbed The Ghost which seemingly expanded her transformation ability and lets her use her meany armed form, this also gave her the ability to summon and use The Ghost’s hands.

KAMI absorbed The Chef gained his powers this allowed her to change colours and fully shapeshift, according to her code she also got “INTUITION” from him, it is not clear what this means but it likely refers to the visons The Chef has so this likely gives her a limited form of precognition

KAMI may have received some powers form The Wind as her code seemingly references him but it also claims that “TRANCES ELEMENTS ONLY” only so I don’t think she has significant abilities from this.

Personal Timeline

This segment will be going over all the events of KAMI’s Life in order. I will do this as extensively as possible so some speculation will be included but this will be shown as speculation to the best of my ability.

Below is an explanation for how the timeline works.


All events will be in chronological order if possible.


If multiple events happen in the same year that will be numbered in the order they occur, explanations below for clarity.

Events that happen within an episode not a flashback will have the episode number after the year.

if the order is unclear or there are no other events then there will be no number to signify what the order they occurred in.

in cases where only the order of some events are clear, the events that have no clear order will share a number.


The date is as specific as it is written, explanations below for clarity.

If a year is stated with no additional information that is the exact year it happened.

If a year is said to be before a year it is that year or any point before.

If a year is said to be after a year it is that year or any point after.

If a year is said to be around a year it is near that year but may be a few years before or after.

In Episode 16 we see a calendar with a date on it of November 2015 which implies the series takes places a few years after this.

KAMI’s Interface Information from Episode 24

In this segment I will go over some of the commands being run in KAMI and what they mean.

Segment 1

INITIATE CAMOUFLAGE.CONFIG LIBRARY: AKKOROKAMUI GHOST, INITIATE GUEST.HOST.VOICE.CONFIG LIBRARY: GHOST

This shows that KAMI is using both The Ghost and The Chefs powers to impersonate Henryk’s Greet-Granddaughter.

Segment 2

WARNING: K.A.M.I. MASK DISENGAGED, K.A.M.I MASK REGISTRATION FAILED

These commands would have been run after Mischief stole KAMI’s mask.

Segment 3

WARNING: THREAT DETECTION COMPROMISED,INITIATE SELF.DESTRUCT.CONFIG + MASK, LIBRARY: AKKOROKAMUI, STATUS: COMPLETE

After KAMI’s mask is stolen, she initiates her self-destruction protocol in a final attempt to kill Mischief.

Segment 4

WARNING: INTERNAL C.E. VULNERABLE WARNING: INTERNAL C.E. LEVELS, WARNING: INTERNAL CLE LEVELS DROPPING

This shows that whatever Mischief was doing to KAMI caused her to loss Cerebral Electricity.

Segment 5

EMERGENCY PROCEDURES INITIATED

INITIATE: PING.'GPS.ONFIG + MECHANICAL SACCORHYTUS

This implies that KAMI was programmed to inform The Mechanical Saccorhytus of her location in the event of her imminent destruction.

Segment 6

INITIATE, CHAOS.DESTRUCT.CONFIG, + METALLIC-EXOSKELETON MK. II, LIBRARY: SHIVA, AKKOROKAMUI

This is the final command that was needed to activate herself destruct implying that KAMI was using both her power and the power of The Chef to do so.

KAMI Represents The Loss Of Individuality To Society

Individuality is something that seems particularly important to the series, Mischief is arguably meant to represent individualisation, noticeably in episode 22 quoting The Red Book which is all about individuality.

KAMI how is directly antithetical to Mischief seems to be some form of robot that is powered by the collected Cerebral Electricity of the dead. I think this may be meant to represent the loss of individuality that society often tries to enforce, practically by people who are like Mr Greetings and are obsessed with an idea of how the world is meant to be.
