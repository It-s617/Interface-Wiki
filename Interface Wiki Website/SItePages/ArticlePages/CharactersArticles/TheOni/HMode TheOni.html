Description

Physical Appearance

The Oni strongly resembles an adult human wearing an Oni mask.

Oni masks are originally from Japan and are made to resemble creatures that are usually described as monsters or demons.

The Oni may be red in coloration as this is what they appear as in The Condemnation however this could just be a result of the lighting of the scene.



Personality

The Oni has no observable personality as of yet.

It is possible that their personality will be an invers of KAMI’s as there appearance is strongly reminiscent of a Japanese Oni, Oni’s represent demons in Japan while KAMI usually translates to god which would imply they are opposites.



Note

All information in this article acts under the assumption that this is a variation of KAMI that was created as a result of her self-destruction in episode 24. If this is proven false, basically everything here will be false.

Analysis

Who is The Oni

The Oni appears to have a connection to KAMI, I believe that The Oni is meant to be a transformed version of KAMI after her self destruction ion episode 24 The abyss.

The Oni is an incredibly unique creature even within the interface’s universe. As form what we can tell The Oni is the result of KAMI transforming into a Cerebral Electricity Monster, KAMI being a robot containing a large amount of cerebral electricity presumably from humans.

Given that cerebral electricity monsters seem to have forms heavily based on their memory’s and perspectives on the world this would likely lead to The Oni being incurably unique as they are a composite of many people cerebral electricity that was being contained by KAMI.
