Description

Physical Appearance

Mr Greetings is an extremely old man with white hair that is usually well dressed.


Personality

Mr Greetings is very contemplative commonly monologuing to himself and seems smart but also quite prejudice.

Powers And Abilities

Mr Greetings appears to be a regular elderly human, but it is clear from episode 21 that Mr Greetings is no longer human as he would have been transformed into a cerebral electricity monster, noticeable he has seemingly maintained his human appearance and it is not clear if he got any powers form this.

Other than this he likely only has the abilities of an old man.

Personal Timeline

This segment will be going over all the timeline of The Mechanical Saccorhytus in order. I will do this as extensively as possible so some speculation will be included but this will be shown as speculation to the best of my ability.

Below is an explanation for how the timeline works.


All events will be in chronological order if possible.


If multiple events happen in the same year that will be numbered in the order they occur, explanations below for clarity.

Events that happen within an episode not a flashback will have the episode number after the year.

if the order is unclear or there are no other events then there will be no number to signify what the order they occurred in.

in cases where only the order of some events are clear, the events that have no clear order will share a number.


The date is as specific as it is written, explanations below for clarity.

If a year is stated with no additional information that is the exact year it happened.

If a year is said to be before a year it is that year or any point before.

If a year is said to be after a year it is that year or any point after.

If a year is said to be around a year it is near that year but may be a few years before or after.

Analysis

Mr Greetings Represents the Past

I believe that both Henryk and Mr Greetings represent the past, I believe that Mr Greetings is meant to represent the old people in the world that refuse to except that the world has moved on from when they were young and are scared of the new world.

This is pretty clear throughout the series especially in Episode 21 Restoration where Mr Greetings admits that a lot of people born after 1943 don’t think there’s anything wrong with the new world and don’t understand what the past was like.
