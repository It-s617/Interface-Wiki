Description

Physical Appearance

The Mechanical Saccorhytus is usually a large blimp with a face at the front of it. However, The Mechanical Saccorhytus can transform into another form that heavily resembles a large baby.


Personality

The Mechanical Saccorhytus is usually inanimate but after transforming into a big baby we take on the usually personality of a baby being very hungry and emotional.

Powers And Abilities

In his normal form The Mechanical Saccorhytus will be able to do anything a blimp can do and can seemingly absorb and store cerebral electricity.

In his baby form he seems much more capable of absorbing cerebral electricity and seems massively strong even being able to pick up a lighthouse, he doesn’t portray any special abilities beyond that however.

The Mechanical Saccorhytus may be able to uses the abilities of the cerebral electricity monsters like KAMI as in episode 12 and 18 it looks like he uses The Ghost powers, but it could just be KAMI doing this of screen.

Analysis

Why Have KAMI When The Mechanical Saccorhytus Is So Much Stronger

During her fight with The Chef, we see that The Chef was capable of beating KAMI before The Mechanical Saccorhytus using The Ghosts powers appears behind him capturing him. We also see that after KAMI self-destruction The Mechanical Saccorhytus is sent in to capture Mischief, this leads to the question why Greetings Robotics needed KAMI.

I think this is simply because KAMI is easier to control and can be relied upon while The Mechanical Saccorhytus is unreliable. When The Mechanical Saccorhytus is in his baby form he is massively emotionally and seams easily distracted likely meaning that he is not reliable for capturing other Cerebral electricity monsters.


Unclear Personal Details
Gender Presentation: none, Unclear

The Mechanical Saccorhytus has two forms, a baby and a blimp never have a gendered appearance.

The Mechanical Saccorhytus appears to be a robotic blimp animated by cerebral electricity, meaning whatever conscience they have is likely a composite of the memories contained in the cerebral electricity which would likely lead it to having a difficult time understanding itself enough similar to a baby. Thia is why I don’t think they have a meaningful gender at all.


Approximate Age: Unclear

It is entirely unclear when The Mechanical Saccorhytus was made, as there is no information given on this over than we know it would have to be after The Philadelphia Experiment during 1943, it is likely that he was bult shortly before The Interface series in 2017, similar to KAMI.


Status: Unclear

After Mischief is consumed by The Mechanical Saccorhytus it slows down and then collapses, appearing dead but this is never confirmed in the series. During safe mode we see that Mischief had forced the Interfaces and presumably The Mechanical Saccorhytus into its safe mode which could explain why it collapses, making it unclear if they are dead at the ends of the series.


Species: Unclear (Cerebral Electricity Container)

The Mechanical Saccorhytus doesn’t appear to be a cerebral electricity monster as it has not transformed from a human and still seams animated by pure cerebral electricity. The Mechanical Saccorhytus appears to just be a blimp that is being powered and animated by cerebral electricity, I have decide to call devices like this “Cerebral Electricity Container”, but I will put unclear as nothing is confirmed in the series.
