This segment will be going over all the timeline of Henryk’s great-granddaughter in order. I will do this as extensively as possible so some speculation will be included but this will be shown as speculation to the best of my ability.

Below is an explanation for how the timeline works.


All events will be in chronological order if possible.


If multiple events happen in the same year that will be numbered in the order they occur, explanations below for clarity.

Events that happen within an episode not a flashback will have the episode number after the year.

if the order is unclear or there are no other events then there will be no number to signify what the order they occurred in.

in cases where only the order of some events are clear, the events that have no clear order will share a number.


The date is as specific as it is written, explanations below for clarity.

If a year is stated with no additional information that is the exact year it happened.

If a year is said to be before a year it is that year or any point before.

If a year is said to be after a year it is that year or any point after.

If a year is said to be around a year it is near that year but may be a few years before or after.

Justification

We know Henryks Great-Granddaughter was still a baby around 1 to 3 years old in 2015 as this is the date of the crash, given that the series is believed to be set in 2017 then it would make her 3 years old at youngest and 5 years old at oldest. If she is 5 years old this would also roughly match the appearance of Henryk’s Great-Granddaughter.

Given that The Museum Boy appears the same age as Henryk’s Great-Granddaughter this can be used to approximate his age.

We see the photo of Henryk’s family has both his daughter and great-granddaughter meaning that Henryk’s daughter must die after Henryk’s Great-Granddaughter was born, given that Henryk’s Great-Granddaughter is still a bay during the crash she this would be about at the similar time.

Justification

My reasoning for these events happening in 2015 is the calendar we see in episode 16 where we see a calendar in Henryk’s abandoned house dated November 2015 it is odd for the scene to focus on this for no reason, so I think it has given a hint to when the home was abandoned.

Analysis

Is She a Willing Hostage

In Episode 21 she is shown to be much happier as her old apartment building is restored, she then walks away and is seemingly greeted by The Ghost, this seemingly implies she was tricked by Greetings Robotics into thinking that The Ghost was willingly working with them.

However in episode 24 she is clearly uncomfortable and runs away as soon as possible so she has likely figured out that this was only a copy of The Ghost being used by Greetings Robotics.

If I am right about her having been tricked, she would not be a willing hostage but would have likely gone with them willingly at first.
