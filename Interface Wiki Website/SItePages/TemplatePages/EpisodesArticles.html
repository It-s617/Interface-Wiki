Description

This is the first episode in the series that introduces the main characters, the episode also introduces some of the most important aspects of the series like The Philadelphia Experiment and Timeline.

This episode is also when Henryk and Mischief meet for the first time with Mischief discussing his past with The Philadelphia Experiment.

Summary

Opening
Henryk is standing outside a store when suddenly Mischief appears behind him. Mischief pokes Henryk causing him to become startled and drop his cigarette, the cigarette is shown twirling as it falls.

Mischief’s story
Mischief begins to explain the history of The Philadelphia Experiment. Mischief explains that the ship he was stationed on was docked in Philadelphia and it was equipped with a electromagnetic field generator that was meant to make the ship invisible, however it instead teleported them to New York. The ship teleporting to New York had caused the crew of the ship to fuse with the hull of the ship and destroyed Mischief’s physical form. Mischief stats that he was forced to regain his physicality but does not specify how.

Ending
The cigarette mentioned earlier is seen and finishes falling. Henryk appears shocked by Mischief before the episode abruptly ends.

Notes

This episode likely takes places in Montral. (speculation)

This is the first episode that introduces Mischief, Henryk, Henryk’s Cigarettes, Timeline, and The Philadelphia Experiment.
